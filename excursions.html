TULUM Historical Tour
Take a tour of the Historial ruins, just 1 Hour from the Grand Velas Resort. Experience the only walled city built by the Mayans along the Caribbean. This ancient fortress rising on top of a cliff overlooking the coast, so formidable in its day when the Spanish by-passed the area on their way to conquering the peninsula.

The views are spectacular and the site offers a unique look back in time. Several structures still have original Mayan paintings on the walls.

Sign up and make this your optional activity.

Limit one per attendee.

Grand Coral GOLF
Located on the beautiful Riviera Maya the golf course “Grand Coral Golf Riviera Maya” is where Nick Price, renowned golf course designer and legendary hall of fame golfer has created 18 a hole, par 71 championship golf course that winds its way through a magnificent natural jungle overlooking deep blue lakes. Admired for his ability to create golf courses that are fun to play, Price has created a masterpiece that will appeal to golfers of all skill levels.

Test your skills and enjoy a day on the course with fellow Image Tech attendees by choosing this optional activity.

Limit one per attendee.

Fun on the WATER
Experience a fun adventure called the "Jungle Tour" where you can manage your own boat, offering two activities in one tour. First you are assigned your personal motor boat, fit for two, traveling about 45 minutes on the waterways by the majestic mangroves of Luguna de Nichupte, on your way to the second largest barrier reef in the world. Then spend about 45 minutes snorkeling at Cancun's National Marine Park to admire multicolored fish and delicate coral formations. This is a very popular half day tour, not to miss!

Included in this optional activity is round trip transportation from Grand Velas, snorkeling equipment, brand new snorkel tubes to keep as a gift, bottled water after snorkeling, lockers for personal items you don't wish to get wet, cooler on the bus with drinks and dry snacks, and all gratuities and fees. Don't forget to wear your bathing suit, bring your biodegradable sun block, sunglasses and a towel. Must be 18 years and older to operate the boat and 16 and older to attend.

If Fun on the Water is your game, sign up for this optional activity!
Limit one per attendee.
